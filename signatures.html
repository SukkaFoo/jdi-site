How to install in Outlook 365 online

  1. Click inside the white signature card below, press Ctrl+A to select everything inside it, then Ctrl+C to copy.
  2. In Outlook online: Settings (⚙) → View all Outlook settings → Mail → Compose and reply
  3. Click New signature, give it a name, then press Ctrl+V to paste.
  4. Replace any placeholder values — phone numbers are marked +X (XXX) XXX-XXXX or +27 (XX) XXX-XXXX, and LinkedIn links say your-handle.
  5. Click Save, then set the signature as your default if prompted.
